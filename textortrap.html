Phishing
Phishing is when a scammer pretends to be someone you trust — like a bank, delivery service, or a friend — to trick you into clicking a fake link or giving away personal information.
Real banks never send links asking you to log in by text.
Urgency ("Act NOW! Expires in 10 min!") is a classic scam tactic.
Always check the URL — scammers use fakes like "amaz0n.com".
If you didn't enter a contest, you can't win one.
Never share your password with anyone, ever!
